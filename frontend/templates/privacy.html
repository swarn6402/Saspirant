Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to provide alert services. This includes your name, email address, date of birth, highest qualification, exam preferences, and monitored website URLs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to match notifications with your preferences, send relevant job or exam alerts, and show your dashboard history and activity.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account, preferences, and notification data are stored in PostgreSQL hosted on Neon. We use reasonable technical and operational controls to protect stored data.

4. Email Delivery

We use SendGrid to deliver email alerts. Your email address and alert content are processed through SendGrid only for delivering notifications and service-related messages.

5. Your Rights

You can update your preferences any time from your dashboard. You may also request account deletion and data removal by contacting us.

If you believe any information is inaccurate, contact us to correct it.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as features evolve. When we make major changes, we will update the “Last updated” date on this page.
